Do you have 15-20 free minutes and a stable internet connection? Want to connect with and inspire a diverse set of undergraduate and graduate students in the computer science and data science majors?

Sign up to do a virtual AMA live for one of my courses!

Content

I'll ask you to spend ~5 minutes introducing yourself to the class, and then will leave the remaining ~10-15 minutes for students to ask questions.

I'll send out your biography ahead of time to the students, and will ask each student to introduce themselves before asking their question.

I recommend you have your favorite visualization (or two) ready to show off. Other than that, you should not need to do any prep ahead of time.

Logistics

The AMAs will be conducted virtually via Zoom (free), so no travel is required. The room link will be setup ahead of time, allowing you to test your setup. The AMA can optionally be recorded or livestreamed—that is completely up to you!

I can accommodate an AMA at the start of most lectures (except for activity or presentation days). The lecture schedule is:

  • Tuesdays, Thursdays at 12:45pm – 2:30pm

I can suggest specific dates/times if you are interested.
